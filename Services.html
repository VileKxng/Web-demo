Understanding Dialysis Treatment

What is Dialysis?

Dialysis is a life-saving treatment that removes waste products and excess fluid from the blood when the kidneys are no longer able to perform these functions effectively. This process is essential for patients with kidney failure to maintain their health and quality of life.

Types of Treatment

  • Hemodialysis
  • Peritoneal Dialysis
  • Home Dialysis Support
  • Emergency Dialysis

Maintaining Kidney Health

Diet Management

  • Control sodium intake
  • Monitor protein consumption
  • Limit phosphorus and potassium
  • Stay hydrated appropriately

Lifestyle Changes

  • Regular exercise
  • Stress management
  • Adequate sleep
  • Blood pressure monitoring

Regular Check-ups

  • Regular blood tests
  • Medication management
  • Blood pressure checks
  • Dietary consultations
